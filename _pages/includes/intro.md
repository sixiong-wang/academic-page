# 👨‍🎓 Profile
- I graduated from [AA DRL](https://drl.aaschool.ac.uk/) program in March 2026 with a Master of Architecture degree, after completing my Bachelor of Architecture at [Chang'an University](https://jzx.chd.edu.cn/).

<br>I currently work at [Minimaforms](https://minimaforms.com/), where I collaborate with my thesis tutor, Dr. Theodore Spyropoulos, Director of AA DRL.

<br>I have gained valuable practical experience, and I am keen to develop in my hometown, Hainan, in a way that combines diverse architectural self-reflection with local culture. I previously worked at [Hainan Provincial Design and Research Institute Co., Ltd](https://www.hniad.com/html/index.html), and interned at [Shenzhen Huahui Design Co., Ltd](http://www.hhd-sz.com/). There, I delved into real estate finance models shaped by a delirious development context with a structural yet invisible social force, contributing to the project "**Jumping Funland: Pressure Evacuation Palace**". Furthermore, during the pandemic, I worked for over a year in a hotel management and service company, while continually delving into architectural theory.

<br>In the pursuit of design progress, I actively participated in numerous academic workshops worldwide, receiving guidance from professionals such as [Nate Hume from UPenn](https://www.design.upenn.edu/people/nate-hume), [Lifeng Lin from LIN Architecture](https://lin.archi/about), and [Yu Yan from SAUP](https://saup.szu.edu.cn/info/1094/2367.htm).


- My passion for architecture has never waned, and my design consciousness continuously focuses on the abstract "**movement**" of architecture: externally, how buildings narrate through silent forms; and internally, how static spaces guide dynamic behaviors of participants.
